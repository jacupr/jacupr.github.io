Work History

I have had the privilege of working in various roles across different industries, each of which has given me valuable experience and skills.


Currently, I am working as an AFE Packer at Amazon Warehouse in Garner, NC. Since January 2023, I have been responsible for packing orders into designated boxes/bags, ensuring accuracy and timeliness in meeting customer demands.


Prior to this, I worked as a Ham Conversion specialist at Smithfield Foods in Tar Heel, NC, from July 2022 to December 2022. In this role, I cut the ham off the pig's leg, ensuring the quality of the meat


Before that, I worked at Mueller Water Products in Saint Pauls, NC, as an OEM specialist from March 2022 to July 2022. Here, I used my skills in brazing and soldering to work with copper pipes. I also water tested and packed them, ensuring they met the required quality standards.


Unfortunately, the department moved to Mexico, and I had to find a new job. I then worked as a Cut Floor specialist at Smithfield Foods in Tar Heel, NC, from July 2021 to February 2022. In this role, I was responsible for cutting the shoulder bone out of the pig, ensuring the meat was prepared correctly.


Before that, I worked as a Live Hanger at Wayne-Sanderson Farms in Saint Pauls, NC, from March 2021 to July 2021. In this role, I live hung 28 chickens a minute, ensuring that they were handled humanely and efficiently.


Finally, I worked as a Warehouse Associate at Walmart in Davenport, FL, from August 2020 to February 2021. Here, I was responsible for unloading and receiving packages from trucks, ensuring they were stored and managed efficiently.


Overall, my work history demonstrates my ability to learn quickly, adapt to new environments, and develop skills in a range of industries.
